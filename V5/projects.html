Robotics · UMD Loop
3-DOF Differential Wrist

Developed and prototyped a spherical, three degrees of freedom differential wrist for a robotic arm, capable of uninhibited 360° motion using a combination of three Neo 550 motors on separate input shafts.

Incorporated a fully-custom bevel gear generator to produce two sets of unique 55° bevel gears and a planar crown gear for consistent and fluid movement along all axes of rotation.

Nerf Gun Controller

Designed and built a custom Nerf gun controller from scratch — integrating a custom-fabricated housing, trigger mechanisms, and electronic firing control using Arduino circuitry.

No off-the-shelf controller components. Full mechanical and electrical design: 3D-printed housing, wired trigger switch, MOSFET-driven motor control, and a custom PCB layout.
